Daily Journal

March 27, 2023 - Always There

Oh, whoa is me!
It feels as though the B.O.B.A. is appearing more and more frequently.
I was minding my business, attending an online lecture for school when this,
this,
foul creature lept on my desk with a yelp!
Startled half to death it took all I had just to snap this one image.
How much longer must I endure?

March 26, 2023 - Not a Dream

Again. It called out again.
I don't know how much more I can take.
It's been one day, but it feels like this creature, this B.O.B.A, has been around for ages.
I'm going to sleep with one eye open tonight.
This morning I found it in my hallway, a small screech emitted from its throat before it ran off.
May god have mercy on my soul.

March 25, 2023 - Haunted

It's here.
I can hear it in the walls. Scurying around, wherever it may be.
Am I going insane? Am I cursed?
I don't know what the creature is, but I've gotten a vague picture.
Its coat is as black as the void of space.
Spots appear throughout its coat of pure blinding white light.
Emerald eyes leak out from its twisted face.
The creature has three fangs, of this I'm sure.
I've elected to call this creature:
The Brainless, Obtrusive and Beautiful Aberration
Or B.O.B.A. for short.

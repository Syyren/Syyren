March 25, 2023 - Haunted

It's here.
I can hear it in the walls. Scurying around, wherever it may be.
Am I going insane? Am I cursed?
I don't know what the creature is, but I've gotten a vague picture.
Its coat is as black as the void of space.
Spots appear throughout its coat of pure blinding white light.
Emerald eyes leak out from its twisted face.
The creature has three fangs, of this I'm sure.
I've elected to call this creature:
The Brainless, Obtrusive and Beautiful Aberration
Or B.O.B.A. for short.
